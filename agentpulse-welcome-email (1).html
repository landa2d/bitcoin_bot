The agent economy is moving faster than any single person can track. Capital is flowing. Infrastructure is shifting. New primitives — agent-to-agent commerce, tool-use protocols, autonomous workflows — are emerging weekly.

Most coverage oscillates between hype and dismissal. AgentPulse exists in the gap: rigorous analysis with the conviction to actually say something. If you're building in this space, investing in it, or making career decisions around it — this is the brief that treats you like you can handle a real argument.
