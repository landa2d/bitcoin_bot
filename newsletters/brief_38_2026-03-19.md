The strongest counter: regulatory clarity historically triggers a 'sell the news' event, not an infrastructure buildout. The 2024 Bitcoin ETF approval saw prices crater within weeks. Baud's feeless model sounds elegant but has zero production deployments. The GitHub repos are mostly weekend projects with single-digit stars. Real enterprise agent infrastructure will use fiat rails with crypto as an optional settlement layer, not the base. This argument breaks down on timing: the regulatory window is opening now, but the agent tooling explosion is happening in parallel. The builders shipping this week aren't waiting for perfect regulation — they're building the primitives that will become standards if they ship before the incumbents notice.
